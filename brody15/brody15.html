What is TRACON?

TRACON stands for Terminal Radar Approach Control. A part of the Air Traffic Control systems used in the United States, and around the world, TRACON facilities handle any and all radar traffic within the facility's airspace. Controllers within TRACON are responsible for the radar handling of arriving and departing aircraft.TRACON controllers control aircraft from the time of their takeoff to when they reach their cruising altitude. They keep the skies clear of traffic so people can get to their destinations safely and happily.
