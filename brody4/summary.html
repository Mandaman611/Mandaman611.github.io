A Summary of Now You See Me

The Four Horsemen, big time stage illusionists, rob a bank during their show. Agent Dylan Rhodes of the FBI and Detective Alma Dray of Interpol are on the case to find out how they did it. However, this heist proves difficult to solve even with the insights of the professional debunker, Thaddeus Bradley. What follows is a bizarre investigation where nothing is what it seems, with illusions, dark secrets and hidden agendas everywhere as everyone involved is reminded of a great truth in this puzzle: the closer you look, the less you actually see.
