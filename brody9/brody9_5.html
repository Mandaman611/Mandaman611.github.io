History of the United States Navy

The 1900's

  • 1910-1919
    • March 3, 1915
      • The Navy Reserve is created aftar a long campaign by Secretary of the Navy Josephus Daniels and his assistant, future president Franklin D. Roosevelt.
    • November 17, 1917
      • The USS Fanning and USS Nicholson win the first US Naval victory of World War I.
      • German U-boat U-58 is sunk.
    • May 8, 1918
      • The final naval engagement of World War I.
      • Another German U-boat is sunk.
  • 1940-1949
    • December 7,1941
      • Pearl Harbor is attacked by the Japanese.
      • The United States enters the war.
    • June 4-7, 1942
      • The Battle of Midway
      • A major US naval victory in World War II.
    • September 2, 1945
      • The Japanese surrender on the deck of the USS Missouri.
