Geometry

Description:

Taught by Mrs. Yu, geometry builds on concepts developed in the middle school math courses. Focuses include proofs, area and volume, angle/side relationships and in depth examinations of triangles, quadrilaterals and other figures, including how to construct many of these figures using a compass and straightedege.
